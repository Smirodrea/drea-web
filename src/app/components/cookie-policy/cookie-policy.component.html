Our Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data that help us provide better services and a more personalized experience on our website. They also enable us to understand how you interact with our platform.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and expire once you close your browser. They assist in navigation and basic site functionality.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device between visits and help us remember your preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

We collaborate with Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about visitor activity. This helps us improve the user experience and the overall effectiveness of our website.

Managing Cookies

If you prefer, you can adjust your browser settings to reject cookies. However, doing so may affect certain features of our website.

Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this policy, so it's a good idea to revisit it from time to time.
